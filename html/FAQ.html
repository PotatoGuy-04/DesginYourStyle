FAQs

What shipping methods do we use?

We use "bpost" as our shipping method. We strive to provide fast and reliable shipping services to all our customers. You can track your order using the tracking number provided in the confirmation email.

Do we ship outside of Belguim?

we ship to all European Union countries. We are committed to providing our products to a wider audience, and we are constantly working on expanding our shipping options.

What payment methods do we accept?

For now, we only accept PayPal as a payment method. We understand that this may be inconvenient for some customers, and we are working on adding more payment options in the near future. In the meantime, we appreciate your understanding and support.

Do we make high-quality products?

Yes, we make high-quality products. We use premium materials and advanced manufacturing techniques to ensure that our products meet the highest standards of quality and durability. We are committed to providing our customers with products that they can rely on and enjoy for a long time.

What products do we sell?

For now, we only sell small amount of products, such as t-shirts, mugs, and tote bags. However, we are planning to expand our product range in the near future. We are constantly working on adding new and exciting products to our website, and we welcome your suggestions and feedback. If you would like to see more of our products, please follow us on social media and stay tuned for updates.
